How the line works

1

Each card is a unit. The pills are its streams, in kg/h.

2

Conservation of mass. At steady state nothing piles up, so every card must balance: left total = right total (in = out).

3

A stream shared by two units shows up in both cards — fill it once. A recycle card shows its ratio so you can crack the loop.

4

Fill every blank so all units go green. One solution, only mental arithmetic.
