How to lay the garden: the board is divided into glazed plots (the coloured patches). A plot of n tiles holds the numbers 1 to n, each exactly once. And the gardener's rule: identical numbers may never touch — not even diagonally. No rows, no columns, no boxes. This is not sudoku; the plots and the touch rule do all the work.
